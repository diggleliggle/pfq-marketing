Qualifications

Built around competence,
not just knowledge.

Every IPP qualification combines formal assessment with real evidence of project practice. The result is a credential that tells an employer something meaningful.

Qualification pathway

1

Foundation

IPP Foundation Certificate in Project Management FCPM

The entry-level qualification. Delivered on demand via Activate. No exam centre, no booking window. Sit when you are ready.

Scenario questions make up at least 30% of the paper, so the assessment reflects real project judgement, not just textbook recall.

Format

40 questions, 60 minutes

Pass mark

75% — published

AIPP membership

Practitioner

IPP Practitioner Certificate in Project Management PCPM

For project professionals with one to three years of active delivery experience. Combines a written assessment with a portfolio of real project evidence submitted via Calibrate.

Two assessors review each submission independently. A third moderates. The rigour is genuine.

Written paper

90 minutes, 2 scenarios

Portfolio

6 of 8 competency areas

Leads to

MIPP membership

Register interest

Assessment fee

£195

Portfolio reviewed within 15 working days

Equivalent to: APM PMQ, PMP (broadly)

3

Advanced

IPP Advanced Certificate in Project Leadership ACPL

For senior practitioners moving into leadership, governance, or programme management. Project Leadership rather than Project Management in the title is deliberate.

A 60-minute structured professional discussion with two IPP assessors, built around a pre-submitted case study of a complex project you have led. Combined with a full evidence portfolio and reflective statement.

Discussion

60 min, 2 assessors

Portfolio

All 8 competency areas

4

Chartered · ChPP

Coming 2028

Chartered Project Professional

The ChPP is the capstone designation. It is not bought with a fee and an exam. It is earned through a career: a track record, peer endorsement, a rigorous panel assessment, and a commitment to ongoing contribution to the profession. We will not rush it.

Indicative requirements: 7-10 years senior experience, MIPP or FIPP for 3+ years, panel review, peer endorsement.
