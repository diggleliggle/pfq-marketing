The problem

Most organisations manage project capability by instinct and assumption

CPD records live in spreadsheets. Competency assessments are done in annual reviews and forgotten. When someone moves roles, their record disappears entirely.

Visibility

You cannot see what your team can actually do

Job titles and years of experience say nothing about genuine capability. When a complex programme lands, you are guessing who can lead it. Calibrate gives each person a validated competency profile across eight structured areas, evidenced and reviewed inside your organisation.

Mobility

When people move, their capability record disappears

Someone transfers to a new team or joins from outside. Their new manager starts from scratch. With Calibrate, verified competency records follow people across teams and employers automatically. No handover notes. No re-assessment.

Compliance

CPD is tracked on spreadsheets or not at all

If your project managers hold IPP membership, they have mandatory CPD obligations. Even if they do not, professional development matters. Calibrate makes CPD visible at team level without chasing anyone for certificates.

What Calibrate does

Everything your team needs to track, evidence, and develop project capability

Built for project management teams specifically. Not a generic LMS with CPD bolted on.

01

Validated competency framework tracking

Each practitioner self-assesses against the IPP framework across eight competency areas. Designated reviewers inside your organisation validate the evidence. What you see on the team dashboard is confirmed, not self-reported. Heat maps show validated levels by area and by role benchmark, so gap analysis is automatic.

02

CPD logging with evidence and compliance reporting

Practitioners log CPD activities with supporting evidence and categories. Hours are tracked against annual targets. The admin sees compliance status across the whole team without chasing anyone. Audit-ready records, timestamped and categorised, available at individual and team level.

03

Internal reviewer workflows and validation queue

You nominate reviewers within your organisation. Evidence submissions go into a structured validation queue, not an email inbox. Reviewers confirm or return submissions with notes. The validation chain is inside Calibrate and fully auditable. No external sign-off required.

04

Shareable practitioner profiles with portability

Each practitioner builds a portable professional profile showing validated competency levels, CPD progress and IPP credentials. Profiles belong to the individual, not the organisation. When someone moves roles or changes employer, their professional record moves with them from day one.

Frequently asked questions

Common questions

What is Calibrate?
Calibrate is an enhanced competency management system built for project management teams. It tracks validated competency levels across eight IPP framework areas, manages CPD compliance, and provides team-level reporting and evidence portfolios. It is seat-licensed, organisation-provisioned, and backed by the Institute of Project Practitioners professional framework.
How much does Calibrate cost?
Calibrate is priced per seat per year. Starter (1 to 10 seats) is £49 per seat. Team (11 to 50) is £39. Corporate (51 to 200) is £29. Enterprise (200 or more) is £22. A 30-day free trial with 5 seats is available with no credit card required. You can also buy seats directly without a trial if you are ready to go.
Is Calibrate linked to IPP membership?
Yes. Calibrate is built on the IPP Competency Framework, the professional standard used by the Institute of Project Practitioners for qualification and membership. CPD logged in Calibrate contributes toward IPP membership obligations. Team members who pass Activate qualifications have their competency scores automatically pre-seeded into their Calibrate profile.
What competency areas does Calibrate cover?
Calibrate tracks competency across eight IPP framework areas: Governance and Lifecycle, Stakeholder and Communications, Leadership and People, Requirements and Scope, Planning and Scheduling, Budget, Cost and Commercial, Risk, Issue and Change Control, and Quality, Assurance and Closure. Each area uses a five-level proficiency scale from Aware to Expert.
Can staff take their Calibrate profile when they leave?
Yes. Competency records and CPD evidence belong to the individual, not the organisation. When someone leaves, their professional profile stays with them. When they join a new employer using Calibrate, their verified record is already in place from day one.
Does Calibrate replace our existing LMS or HR system?
No. Calibrate sits alongside existing systems as the professional competency and CPD record layer. It captures evidence of what practitioners have demonstrated and validates it through internal reviewers. It does not replace your training programmes, LMS, or performance management platform.
How long does it take to set up?
Your organisation is provisioned in seconds when you start a trial or buy seats. There is no implementation project, no data migration, and no training sessions. The IPP competency framework is already built in. Your team can join using their existing IPP or Activate account, or create a free account when they receive their invite.
