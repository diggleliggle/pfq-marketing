The problem

Most teams can't answer basic questions about their project capability

CPD records live in spreadsheets. Competency assessments are done in annual reviews and forgotten. Skill gaps are visible only after something goes wrong.

No single source of truth

CPD logs, training records, and competency assessments scattered across email, spreadsheets, and HR systems. Audit-ready evidence is almost impossible to pull together quickly.

Skill gaps only visible in hindsight

Without ongoing competency tracking, organisations find out about gaps when a project struggles, a bid is lost, or a key person leaves. By then it is too late to act.

CPD disconnected from careers

Hours logged in one system, qualifications tracked somewhere else, professional development happening in isolation. Practitioners can't demonstrate their growth in a way that means anything.

What Calibrate does

Everything your team needs to track, evidence, and develop project capability

Built for project management teams. Not a generic LMS with CPD bolted on.

01

Competency framework tracking

Each practitioner self-assesses against a structured project management competency framework covering eight areas. Managers review and validate assessments, creating a verified, evidence-based record of capability across the team.

02

CPD logging with evidence

Practitioners log CPD activities with supporting evidence — documents, links, notes. Hours are tracked against annual targets. Everything is timestamped and audit-ready, with categories covering formal learning, on-the-job development, coaching, and conferences.

03

Team-level reporting

L&D and HR leads get a team dashboard showing competency heat maps, CPD compliance rates, and validation status across the group. Identify who is below target, where the gaps sit, and where training investment should go next.

04

Shareable practitioner profiles

Each practitioner builds a portable professional profile showing their competency levels, CPD record, and IPP credentials. Shareable with employers, clients, and professional bodies. Useful for bids, secondments, and career progression conversations.

Why Calibrate is different

The only CPD platform linked to a project management professional body

Generic CPD software tracks hours. Calibrate connects development directly to a recognised professional credential — so the work your team puts in actually means something beyond your organisation.

Linked to IPP credentials

Calibrate is built alongside the Institute of Project Practitioners. CPD logged in Calibrate contributes toward IPP membership requirements. Practitioners who progress through Activate and earn qualifications see that recognised in their Calibrate profile.

Built for project management specifically

The competency framework is designed for project practitioners, not adapted from a generic HR tool. Eight competency areas covering delivery, stakeholder management, governance, risk, and more — mapped to how project work actually happens.

Portable records that belong to the practitioner

CPD records and competency evidence stay with the individual, not the organisation. When someone moves roles or changes employer, their professional record moves with them. This makes Calibrate a benefit practitioners actually value, not just a compliance tool.

Works alongside your existing L&D

Calibrate does not replace your existing training programmes or LMS. It sits alongside them as the professional record layer — capturing evidence of what practitioners have done and validating what they can demonstrate, whatever the source of that learning.

Who it's for

Built for the people responsible for team capability

Calibrate is seat-licensed and organisation-provisioned. One platform, three perspectives.

L&D leads

See where development is actually landing

Move beyond completion rates. Calibrate shows you whether training is translating into demonstrated competency — validated by managers, evidenced by practitioners, and visible at team level. Plan your next investment with data, not instinct.

HR teams

CPD compliance without the admin burden

Maintain audit-ready CPD records across your project management population without chasing spreadsheets. Calibrate tracks hours, categories, and evidence in one place — and ties it to professional standards that mean something outside your organisation.

Operations leads

Know what your team can actually deliver

Understand your project capability before you commit to a bid or assign a team. Competency heat maps show you where your team is strong and where there are gaps — so you can resource projects with confidence and develop deliberately.
