About us

Who we are

Module Six is a UK company building professional infrastructure for project practitioners. We created IPP, Activate, and Calibrate because the existing options in the market were either too rigid, too expensive, or too disconnected from how practitioners actually work.

Our products share a single platform. One account gives a practitioner access to their learning, their credentials, and their CPD record. Organisations using Calibrate can connect their team's development directly to the qualifications their practitioners hold through IPP.

We are based in the UK and focused on building something practitioners genuinely want to use.
