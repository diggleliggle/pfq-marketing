The problem

The existing landscape is not good enough.

Qualifications that test recall under exam conditions. Credentials that tell an employer what you knew five years ago. Professional bodies that collect a fee and issue a card.

Project management as a discipline deserves better. IPP is built to provide it.

Our approach
The old model

Sit an exam. Get a certificate. Pay your annual fee. Credential never updates.

The IPP model

Evidence-based assessment. Designation that reflects where you are today. Membership that actively supports your development.

How it works

Learn. Qualify. Develop.

Three connected products. One professional pathway. Each reinforces the others. Your progress in one updates your record in all.
