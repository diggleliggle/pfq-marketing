What Groundwork is

More than exam prep.
A foundation for your career.

Groundwork is a structured project management learning platform. The content is built around established practice, drawing on the APM Body of Knowledge and recognised frameworks used across industries.

Work through Groundwork and you will come away with a solid grasp of how projects are defined, planned, delivered, and closed — the frameworks, the thinking, and the applied judgement that underpins effective project work at every level. If you are sitting the APM PFQ, the curriculum maps directly to the syllabus and will prepare you thoroughly for the exam.

Our longer-term ambition is to build this into a recognised learning path in its own right, with our own assessments and qualifications. Groundwork is the beginning of that.

The challenge

Real knowledge takes
time to build.

Project management is a broad discipline. Governance, planning, risk, stakeholder management, benefits realisation — each of these is a subject in its own right. Understanding them properly, not just well enough to answer a question, takes structured effort.

Most people try to fit learning around a full-time job. That means picking things up and putting them down, losing the thread, and never quite feeling confident in the foundations.

Groundwork gives you a clear structure, tested understanding at every step, and a picture of where you actually stand — not just where you think you do.

How it works

Designed to fit around
a working week.

Groundwork is built for people learning alongside a full-time job. Short focused lessons, smart revision, and a clear path from first principles to genuine confidence.

1

Learn

Each topic opens with a clear explanation of the concept, a worked example from a realistic project scenario, and a note on where people commonly misapply it. Lessons are short enough to complete in a single sitting, long enough to actually cover the ground.

2

Practise

After each lesson, answer a set of practice questions. Scenario-based and applied. Feedback on every answer explains the reasoning, so each question is a learning moment, not just a score.

3

Track

Your dashboard shows progress across all six modules, recent scores, and the topics that need more work. You always know where you are and what to do next.

4

Revise

The app automatically resurfaces weak topics for review. Flashcards, targeted questions, quick recaps. No need to maintain your own revision list.

5

Assess

When you're through the modules, sit the mock exam under timed conditions. Review your readiness report. If the numbers are there, you have the knowledge — and you're ready to book the exam.
