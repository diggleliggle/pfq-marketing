The challenge

Real knowledge takes
time to build.

Project management is a broad discipline. Governance, planning, risk, stakeholder management, benefits realisation: each of these is a subject in its own right. Understanding them properly, not just well enough to answer a question, takes structured effort.

Most people try to fit learning around a full-time job. That means picking things up and putting them down, losing the thread, and never quite feeling confident in the foundations.

Activate gives you a clear structure, tested understanding at every step, and a picture of where you actually stand, not just where you think you do.

Two ways to learn

Study the way that works for you.

At the start you choose how the app presents information. Same content, same qualification outcome. The difference is how it feels to work through it.

Browse and make connections.

More information on screen at once, cross-links visible throughout, full navigation always open. Suited to learners who like to move between topics, follow their curiosity, and build their own picture of the subject.

One concept per screen, minimal clutter, fewer decisions at each step. Progress is always visible. Navigation is stripped back to what you need. Suited to learners who prefer a clear, linear path and find too many options distracting.

You can switch between modes at any time. Both modes cover identical content and lead to the same qualification outcome.

How it works

Designed to fit around
a working week.

Activate is built for people learning alongside a full-time job. Short focused lessons, smart revision, and a clear path from first principles to genuine confidence.

1

Learn

Each topic opens with a clear explanation of the concept, a worked example from a realistic project scenario, and a note on where people commonly misapply it. Lessons are short enough to complete in a single sitting, long enough to actually cover the ground.

2

Practise

After each lesson, answer a set of practice questions. Scenario-based and applied. Feedback on every answer explains the reasoning, so each question is a learning moment, not just a score.

3

Track

Your dashboard shows progress across all six modules, recent scores, and the topics that need more work. You always know where you are and what to do next.

4

Revise

The app automatically resurfaces weak topics for review. Flashcards, targeted questions, quick recaps. No need to maintain your own revision list.

5

Assess

When you're through the modules, sit the FCPM Practice Assessment under timed conditions. Review your readiness report. If the numbers are there, you have the knowledge, and you're ready for whatever comes next, whether that's the FCPM Assessment or just greater confidence in your practice.
