Associate membership (AIPP)

Your first verified credential. AIPP after your name, directory listing, and a clear path to Member as your experience grows.

Member status (MIPP)

MIPP after your name. Calibrate access included. Priority directory listing, mentoring network, and voting rights in IPP governance.

Entry requirements:

  • Foundation qualification or equivalent
  • Brief professional statement
  • Minimum two years of documented project experience

Applications open at IPP launch. £69/year, includes Calibrate access.

Thank you. We will be in touch when applications open.

Fellow status (FIPP)

Peer-reviewed senior status. FIPP after your name, governance involvement, and recognition as a practitioner who contributes to raising standards in the profession.

Entry requirements:

  • Minimum five years of senior project experience
  • Endorsement from two existing IPP Members or Fellows
  • Calibrate evidence portfolio across all competency areas
  • Written contribution statement

Fellow applications reviewed by the governance board.

Thank you. We will be in touch when Fellow applications open.
