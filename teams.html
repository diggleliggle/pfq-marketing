CPD and professional development

Structured learning that builds
a real CPD record.

Professional CPD frameworks, including APM's, ask five questions: where am I now, where do I want to be, how will I get there, how am I doing, and what next. Activate and Calibrate together give individuals clear, evidenced answers to all five.

Completing Activate constitutes formal structured learning under recognised CPD frameworks. Each topic completed, each practice question answered, and each module checkpoint passed is a recorded, evidenced learning activity. Calibrate captures that evidence alongside self-assessed competency levels, making it usable directly in development conversations and professional body records.

For organisations where staff are working towards professional qualifications or membership, both products provide a documented starting point. Progress from Activate and competency records from Calibrate are exportable, giving individuals the dates, topics, and evidence they need for accurate CPD entries.

CPD question 1
Where am I now?
The exam readiness report maps performance across all 75 topics, showing precisely where each learner is confident and where gaps remain.
CPD question 2
Where do I want to be?
A clear, specific goal: complete Activate, sit a professional qualification, and build the foundation for further development.
CPD question 3
How will I get there?
Six modules, 75 topics, spaced repetition, and a full mock exam. A structured path from first topic to exam day, with nothing left to chance.
CPD question 4
How am I doing?
The admin dashboard gives you and the learner an up-to-date picture of progress at any point. The monthly summary covers the whole cohort.
CPD question 5
What next?
With Activate complete and a Calibrate profile in place, the foundation is there for specialist qualifications and continued structured development. Both products are the starting point.

Inclusion and accessibility

A learning tool that works for
every person on your team.

Project teams are diverse. Module Six is built to reflect that. Every learner gets the same content and the same qualification outcome. What changes is how it is delivered. Learners choose the experience that works for them, without having to declare anything or ask for adjustments.

Focus mode

Delivers content one section at a time with no sidebars, no competing links, and a clear progress indicator throughout. Designed with ADHD and anxiety in mind, but chosen by learners of all kinds who prefer a linear, distraction-free path.

OpenDyslexic font

Learners can switch to OpenDyslexic at any time from their profile. The setting persists across sessions and devices. No forms, no disclosure, no separate process. Just a preference a learner can set and forget.

Background tints

Five background colour options (white, cream, yellow, peach, and pale blue) reduce the visual stress of reading on screen. Particularly useful for learners with Irlen syndrome or visual processing differences.

Audio summaries

Every module has a spoken summary: two to three minutes of clearly narrated key points. Learners who process information better through listening can use audio as their primary revision tool, not an afterthought.

Self-paced, no time pressure

Learners move at their own pace with no countdown timers, no session limits, and no performance warnings in learning mode. The mock exam is timed because the real exam is. Everything else is fully self-directed.

WCAG 2.1 AA compliant

Both the web app and phone PWA are built to WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards: keyboard navigation, screen reader support, sufficient colour contrast, and no reliance on colour alone to convey meaning.

Common questions

What L&D teams usually ask.

Does completing Activate count as formal CPD?
Yes. Completing structured online learning is a recognised CPD activity under most professional frameworks. Learners can record completed topics and modules in their CPD log with dates and assessment scores as supporting evidence. Calibrate provides a dedicated tool for logging and structuring that record.
Can progress records be used in appraisals or competency reviews?
Yes. Activate gives topic-level progress and readiness scores. Calibrate adds self-assessed competency levels, evidence records, and line manager endorsements. Together they give line managers concrete, structured data for performance reviews, PDPs, and competency assessments. Progress data can be exported as CSV for use in your own systems.
Is there a minimum order?
We typically work with teams of five or more. If you need fewer seats, get in touch and we can discuss options.
What happens when a learner finishes Activate?
Their Activate account stays active until the licence expires. They can revisit the revision hub, retake the mock exam, and access their full progress record. Their Calibrate account continues beyond the licence as their personal professional development record.
How does seat reassignment work?
If a team member leaves, reassign their Activate seat to a new starter from the admin dashboard. The new learner inherits the remaining time on that seat. All seats in an order expire on the same date.
What does Calibrate add for organisations?
Calibrate gives you structured competency tracking across the team: self-assessments against 8 areas and 41 elements, evidence submissions, line manager endorsements, and an organisation-wide capability heatmap. It replaces spreadsheet-based skills matrices with something structured and evidence-backed.
Do you offer invoicing?
Yes. All organisation orders are invoiced. We accept BACS on 30-day terms and include a PO number field on the invoice.
How long does setup take?
Once an order is confirmed, we provision your organisation within one working day. Learners can start immediately after accepting their invite. No IT configuration required for most deployments.
What data do you hold on learners?
Name, work email, progress data, and the CPD and evidence records they create in Calibrate. Data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties. Full details are in our privacy policy.
What if we need SSO or a large deployment?
Get in touch. SSO is available for larger orders alongside custom reporting and dedicated onboarding support.
